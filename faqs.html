App Development

We develop both native and cross-platform mobile apps for iOS and Android using frameworks like Flutter, React Native, and native languages such as Swift and Kotlin.

Yes! We offer cross-platform solutions to ensure your app runs smoothly on both iOS and Android, or native development if your project requires platform-specific features.

The development timeline depends on the complexity and features of the app. Typically, a basic app takes 2–3 months, while more complex apps may take 4–6 months or more.

Full Stack Developers

A full stack developer handles both the frontend (user interface) and backend (server-side logic, databases) of a web application, providing end-to-end development solutions.

Our team is proficient in MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), MEAN, Python-Django, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Firebase.

UI/UX Designers

UI (User Interface) focuses on the look and feel of an app or website (colors, typography, buttons), while UX (User Experience) is about how users interact with it—ensuring it's easy, intuitive, and efficient to use.

Our team works with industry-standard tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, Photoshop, and Illustrator for wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity design.

QA Engineers

A QA engineer ensures that the software is free of bugs, performs as expected, and meets the required specifications. They test the product at various stages to ensure quality, functionality, security, and usability.

We offer a wide range of testing services, including: Manual Testing, Automated Testing, Unit Testing, Integration Testing, Performance Testing, Regression Testing, Security Testing

Yes, we use tools like Selenium, Appium, Cypress, and JUnit to automate tests for web and mobile applications, helping to save time and ensure consistent results across different environments.

Our team conducts comprehensive testing, ensuring that both frontend and backend are thoroughly tested for functionality, performance, and security. We verify that APIs, databases, and user interfaces work seamlessly together.
